The Janadriyah National Festival started in 1985 in Saudi Arabia. It is a cultural festival that celebrates the old traditions of the country. The festival lasts for two weeks. It usually begins in February or March.

The event starts with a huge camel race. Visitors watch horse races, falconry, and poetry readings. There is an enormous market at the festival. People buy traditional crafts, clothes, and jewellery. Part 3B: Reading – Travels in Africa

28 marks · Multiple Choice (A, B, C) · 4 marks each

In 1895, Mary Kingsley arrived on the west coast of Africa. She planned to travel up the Ogouèe River in Gabon. She wanted to study the way people lived in that part of the world. She carried a small notebook and a camera.

On 5th June, she left the port and travelled by boat. When she was travelling, the river became narrow. The large boat could not go further. She changed to a small wooden canoe. As she was going up the river, she stopped to look at the animals. She collected 65 different types of fish. Scientists later named three fish after her.

In 1897, Mary wrote a book about her travels. The book is called Travels in Africa. Many people read her book to learn about her incredible journey.
